Global Hunger Statistics

828 Million Affected

As of 2021, approximately 828 million people were affected by hunger globally, marking a significant increase from previous years.

2.8 Billion Unable to Afford Healthy Diets

In 2022, rising food prices and income inequality led to 2.8 billion people being unable to afford a healthy diet.

733 Million Undernourished

In 2023, 733 million people globally suffered from malnutrition, highlighting the escalating crisis of hunger and food insecurity worldwide.
